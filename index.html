Background & Research

Email Scholar GitHub LinkedIn ORCID

I am a Research Assistant at the Insight Research Ireland Centre for Data Analytics, DCU and a Machine Learning Researcher at Etsy, based in Dublin, Ireland. I hold a Google Professional Data Engineer and ML Engineer certification.

My research investigates how large language models reason and decide under uncertainty, using adversarial testing, representational probing, reversal learning paradigms, and Theory-of-Mind frameworks to understand and improve AI alignment. I am dedicated to building rigorous evaluation frameworks for safe and responsible AI systems.

I hold an MSc in Statistics and Sustainability (First Class Honours, E3 Scholarship) from Trinity College Dublin, and dual Bachelor's degrees from Harbin Institute of Technology.
